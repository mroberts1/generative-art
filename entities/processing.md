# Processing

Processing is an open-source programming environment and language designed to facilitate generative art, interactive graphics, and visual design. It has become a cornerstone tool for generative artists, educators, and creative coders.

## Community and Accessibility

Processing exemplifies the broader trend of open-source digital art tools that have enabled widespread participation in generative art practice. Users congregate on dedicated websites where:
- Work is shared and critiqued
- Technical help is offered
- New ideas and techniques are exchanged

While the initial attraction is often the low cost of entry, the social network of users has increasingly become the leading asset attracting generative artists and educators to the platform.

## Role in Generative Art

Processing is widely used for:
- Real-time graphics and animation
- Interactive installations
- Procedural generation experiments
- Educational introduction to generative art concepts

## Historical Context

Processing emerged as part of the broader open-source digital art movement, alongside tools like Pure Data and openFrameworks, all designed to democratize access to generative art creation.
