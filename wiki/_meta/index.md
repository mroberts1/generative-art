- How do complexity science and emergence apply across different generative systems (mechanical, biological, algorithmic)?
- What distinguishes "generative art" from algorithmic design or procedural generation as analytic categories?

## Candidates (awaiting 2nd source)

The vault currently has one source. Every concept candidate is awaiting corroboration per the 2-source rule:

- **Generative Art Definition**: Galanter's systems-based framework. Needs: Margaret Boden *The Creative Mind*, Jon McCormack *Computers and Creativity*, or Mitchell Whitelaw *Metacreation* for corroboration.
- **Autonomy in Generative Systems**: Central to Galanter's work. Needs: a practitioner voice (artist statement) or a critical voice disputing the autonomy framing.
- **Complexity Science Framework**: Galanter's theoretical pillar. Needs: independent treatments from complexity/emergence literature (Kauffman, Holland) OR an art-theory voice critiquing the framework (e.g. Simon Penny's *Making Sense*).
- **First-Generation Computer Art**: Would be the most valuable addition to the vault. Needs: a source grounded in the Stuttgart School (Nees, Nake) or US plotter lineage (Noll, Csuri).
